Departments & Units

Operational Structure

Our zone is organized into departments and units built around training, field operations, spiritual growth, and public outreach.

Training Department

Builds curriculum for leadership camps, officer drills, and specialized trainings across the zone.

Program Unit

Coordinates program logistics, event scheduling, and participant communication.

Operational Units

Below is the full list of operational units in Royal Rangers Nigeria Southwest Zone 2.

Missions and Spiritual Emphasis Unit

Responsible for evangelism, outreach missions, and fostering spiritual growth among rangers and leaders across the zone.

Finance Unit

Oversees budgeting, financial records, fund allocation, and ensures transparency and accountability in all financial matters.

Training Unit

Develops and delivers training programs for officers and rangers, including leadership camps, drills, and skills development.

Provost Unit

Maintains discipline, order, and adherence to regulations during parades, camps, and all official activities.

Administrators Academy & Secretariat

Handles administrative records, correspondence, documentation, and the smooth running of the zone's secretariat.

Media & Communication

Manages media coverage, photography, videography, and content creation for zone events and activities.

Publicity & Mobilisation

Drives public awareness, membership drives, and mobilisation of rangers and volunteers for zone programmes.

Ranking & Promotions Board

Evaluates and processes promotions, rank advancements, and maintains the ranking structure for all officers.

Welfare Unit

Looks after the well-being of rangers, officers, and their families through support programmes and care initiatives.

Intelligence & Surveillance Unit

Gathers and analyses information to support planning, security, and strategic decision-making for zone operations.

Internal Audit

Conducts independent reviews of financial and operational processes to ensure compliance and efficiency.

Medical Unit

Provides first aid, health education, and medical support during camps, events, and zone gatherings.

Band / Music Unit

Leads musical performances, parades, and ceremonial music for zone events and activities.

Sports Unit

Organises sporting activities, tournaments, and physical fitness programmes for rangers and officers.

Ordinance Unit

Manages the procurement, storage, and maintenance of equipment, uniforms, and supplies for the zone.

Legal Unit

Provides legal advice, ensures compliance with regulations, and handles legal matters affecting the zone.
