Zone Programs

Detailed descriptions of the key programs that define our activities in Southwest Zone 2.

Camps

Outdoor and leadership camps designed to strengthen teamwork, resilience, and spiritual character.

Leadership Trainings

Focused officer and youth leadership sessions that prepare participants for effective service.

Drills & Parades

Discipline-oriented drills and ceremonial parades that refine coordination, focus, and group pride.

Study & Spiritual Growth

Programs structured to support Bible knowledge, spiritual formation, and character development.

Bible Quiz Programs

Competitive Bible study events that build scripture knowledge and teamwork.

Spiritual Development

Faith-based sessions and devotionals that encourage daily Christian living for youth and leaders.

Community Service

Public service initiatives that connect the zone to local communities and reinforce values through action.

Community service programs include outreach events, volunteer support, local clean-ups, and mentoring efforts designed to serve the broader society while cultivating servant leadership in our members.
