Building the Next Generation of Christlike Leaders

Evangelizing, Equipping, and Empowering boys and young men across Nigeria's Southwest Zone through faith driven mentoring and leadership development.

Who We Are

Royal Rangers Nigeria Southwest Zone

Royal Rangers is a Christ-centered mentoring ministry that helps boys and young men grow spiritually, mentally, physically, and socially through structured, activity-based programs rooted in the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

"To Evangelize, Equip, and Empower the next generation of Christ-followers and lifelong servant leaders."

We evangelize by creating fellowship and outreach opportunities that introduce boys to Christ. We equip through intentional mentoring, training, and discipleship. We empower by opening doors for leadership and service that build confidence and character.

Our Vision

We envision a generation of Christlike leaders disciplined young men of integrity who strengthen their churches, transform their communities, and carry the torch of faith forward. Through mentorship and discipleship, we are building a legacy of servant leadership across the Southwest Zone and beyond.

Our History

Royal Rangers in Nigeria

1984: Royal Rangers introduced in Nigeria, with Rev. David Nduka Okoro playing a foundational role in bringing the ministry to the nation.
1985: Official recognition granted by the Assemblies of God Nigeria, cementing the ministry's place in the church.
1986: The first Nigerian uniform was adopted, giving the movement a distinct local identity.
1988: Activities expanded strongly in Lagos, reaching more boys and establishing a firm foothold in the Southwest.
1990: Official inauguration in Lagos marked a new chapter of growth and organization.
1999: Nationwide leadership training conducted, raising the standard for commanders and officers across Nigeria.
2000: Transition to the international khaki/brown uniform, aligning Nigeria with the global Royal Rangers movement.
2009: First Southwest Zone Trainers' Conference held, strengthening local leadership capacity.
2015: Rev. I. E. Chilaka appointed National Commander, ushering in a season of renewed vision.
2019: Leadership transition in Southwest Zone, positioning the zone for greater impact.
Our Ideals

The Royal Rangers Code

Eight values that define who we are and shape the character of every Royal Ranger.

Alert

Mentally, physically, and spiritually aware ready for anything.

Clean

Pure in body, mind, and speech honoring God in all we do.

Honest

Men of integrity who do not lie, cheat, or steal.

Courageous

Brave in the face of danger, criticism, or threats.

Loyal

Faithful to God, church, family, outpost, and friends.

Courteous

Polite, kind, and thoughtful in every interaction.

Obedient

Respecting parents, leaders, and those in authority.

Spiritual

Committed to prayer, Scripture, and witnessing for Christ.

The Royal Rangers Motto

"Ready" — Ready for anything. Ready to work, play, serve, worship, live, and obey God's Word.

Our Method

The Mentoring Approach

Learning that sticks — the Hear-See-Do-Teach model.

Hear

Boys receive clear instruction on biblical truths and practical skills.

See

Leaders demonstrate each skill, modeling excellence and Christlike character.

Do

Boys practice hands-on, building confidence through real experience.

Teach

Graduates become mentors themselves, reinforcing their learning by leading others.
